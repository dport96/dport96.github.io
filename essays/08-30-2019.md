If you've read my About Myself essay, you would see that I always thought I would become a pharmacist. I never felt a passion for chemistry, but I did love math, hence the Accounting. So why did I choose MIS?

Since I was a young kid, I would always find myself fascinated with computers, I even loved using typewriters. Volunteering at a doctors office also taught me that I enjoyed data entry and organizing information.

As I learned more of what the Shidler College of Business had to offer, I decided to dip my feet into MIS while I am studying Accounting. I hope to open my eyes to the MIS world in ITM 352. MIS is also a good background for any job as technology is used in a numerous amount of ways in business and ITM can be used in decision making to improve a business' strategies.

I am hoping that by the end of the semester I will officially declare a double major in Accounting and MIS.
