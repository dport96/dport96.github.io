Although with the pandemic and a new addition to the family this year I decided to take on another major of MIS.

In order to do this I was told that I needed to start in ITM 352 as soon as possible because I only had 2 semesters left, and now 3 with my newfound major! 

I took on this course and major because although ICS was one of the most trying courses I ever took, I did get a great sense of pride from being able to complete my assignments and course with new knowledge in a subject I knew would be very valuable in the future. Not just personally but professionally.

In many of my other ECON courses we talk about how technology is killing jobs and it only made sense to me to make sure that I would have an advantage in this not so distant future by furthering my education and acquiring another degree in MIS.

I’ve always loved technology ever since I was in middle school creating my own Myspace html design for WAY too many hours throughout the night. I look forward to being able to help my friends and family with their tech related issues and questions in the future!
